# Replace a Wall-Mounted Air Filter Safely

Scene 1
Narration:
"Before you open the filter cabinet, power down the unit and confirm the fan has stopped."
Visual:
Technician stands at a wall-mounted ventilation unit, presses the power control, and waits for the airflow to stop before touching the access panel.

Scene 2
Narration:
"Put on gloves, open the cabinet slowly, and slide the used filter straight out to avoid releasing trapped dust."
Visual:
Gloved hands loosen the latch, open the cabinet carefully, and remove the used filter in one controlled motion while keeping the dirty side facing away.

Scene 3
Narration:
"Check the airflow arrow on the new filter and align it with the arrow marked inside the housing."
Visual:
Close view of the new filter edge and the housing label while the technician rotates the filter until both arrows line up.

Scene 4
Narration:
"Slide the new filter fully into place, close the cabinet, and restore power only after the panel is secured."
Visual:
Technician seats the filter flush, secures the cabinet latch, and then restores power at the control.
